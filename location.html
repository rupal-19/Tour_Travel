MALDIVES

Maldives is well known for its crystalline waters with beautiful shades of blue, swaying palm trees, and sparkling white sand under the limitless blue skies. It is the ultimate dream of luxury and tranquility. ... With good weather throughout most of the year, Maldives makes a great choice for an idyllic beach getaway.

BALI

The best time to visit Bali is between April and October, the island's dry season. Bali experiences only two seasons: the rainy season and the dry season. Throughout the year, the daytime temps hover between the mid-80s and low 90s, with only the humidity and precipitation patterns changing.

PALAWAN

Come to Palawan and discover one of the most amazing natural wonder. The Underground River is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature. The 8.2 km river is the longest underground river in the world, that winds its way underneath a mountain range, through Underground river cave, and then goes out into the South China Sea.
